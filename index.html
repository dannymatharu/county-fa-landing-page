What the dashboard actually gives you

Not a reporting exercise. An operational asset with seven distinct uses.

📊
County-wide social value dataset
Total social value, social value ratios, health and productivity savings and volunteer contribution aggregated across every club that completes an assessment. The county picture Sport England and the Football Foundation want to see when you present to them.
💷
Funding readiness per club
The dashboard automatically flags which clubs are ready to apply for which specific funding pots — Football Foundation, Sport England, National Lottery, local authority grants — based on each club's own deprivation data and infrastructure evidence. You become the organisation that helped them get funded.
🧠
Hidden welfare need across your network
ND and MH prevalence estimates aggregated across your whole county. Not case-by-case — statistically. Your welfare officers get a county-wide picture of likely need rather than waiting for disclosures. No other football social value tool provides this.
📍
Deprivation data by postcode
Every club submission includes an IMD deprivation decile automatically sourced from government data. The dashboard shows which clubs are operating in your most deprived communities — directly relevant to targeted funding eligibility and your case to local authorities.
🏗️
Club infrastructure audit
Welfare officer in post, MH provision, school links, community facility use — across every club that's completed an assessment. Which clubs have welfare infrastructure gaps? Now you know, across the whole county, without asking individually.
📋
Quarterly reports, automatically
The dashboard generates quarterly PDF reports for your board, the FA, Sport England, and local stakeholder presentations. Export CSV at any point. The data is yours — structured and ready for whatever conversation you need to have.

England Football Accredited

Accreditation that comes
with evidence attached.

England Football Accredited status matters. But right now, earning it doesn't produce anything a club can put in a funding application, show to a local authority, or use to attract a sponsor.

With Proof of Play integrated into your EFA programme, accreditation produces a social value report as a direct output. A club that earns EFA status walks away with their estimated annual social value, their social value ratio, and an evidence package built on HM Treasury and Sport England methodology.

That changes what EFA means to a club. And it changes what you can say about your accreditation programme when you're talking to the FA, to Sport England, or to local funders.

How it works

Simple to deploy.
Immediate value for clubs.

01 · You

One licence. Your whole county.

A single annual licence covers every club. You distribute the tool under your own name — clubs associate it with your County FA. No setup cost, no bespoke build, no technical overhead on your end.

Ten minutes. A full report.

Clubs enter their own data — players, volunteers, sessions, programmes. The tool applies published proxy values and generates a complete social value report immediately. No training required, no support needed.

03 · You again

The county picture builds automatically.

Every completion feeds your dashboard. Aggregate social value, social value ratios, health savings, deprivation data, welfare infrastructure, funding readiness — all of it, updated in real time. Quarterly reports generated automatically.

The methodology

Built on the sources
funders already trust.

HM Treasury · WELLBY
Wellbeing-adjusted life years
The UK government's standard for valuing non-market impacts. The same methodology used by NHS interventions and Sport England. £15,900 per WELLBY at 2024 prices.
Sport England · Social Value Model Year 2 (November 2025)
Participant wellbeing values
Sheffield Hallam University, Manchester Metropolitan, State of Life. Active adults: £2,600/yr; disability adults: £5,300/yr; children 11–16: £4,300/yr. The model Football Foundation and Sport England reference.
ONS ASHE 2024 · What Works Centre
Volunteer economic value
Volunteer hours at ONS median hourly wage (£17/hr). Wellbeing value of volunteering at £2,100 per volunteer per year. Most county datasets have never calculated this.
withinu · Unique layer
Neurodiversity & mental health overlay
ND prevalence 10–20% (default 14%) and MH difficulty likelihood 25%. Sources: NHS Digital, British Dyslexia Association, NHS Adult Psychiatric Morbidity Survey 2023/24. No other football social value tool includes this.
